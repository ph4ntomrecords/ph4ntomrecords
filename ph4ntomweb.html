About PH4NTOM Records

PH4NTOM Records is a Kampala-based creative collective operating as a hand-in-hand team of producers, artists, and creatives.

Rather than functioning as a conventional label, the collective emphasizes collaboration, shared growth, and long-term creative development.

The focus remains on authenticity, creative freedom, and building a sustainable ecosystem where sound and identity grow together.
